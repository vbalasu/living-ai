# Human Capabilities Beyond living-ai.md

Beyond the functional list in `living-ai.md`, humans have layers that aren't operationalizable as components.

## Phenomenology
- **Consciousness / qualia** — felt experience, not just state representation. The "what it's like."
- **Emotion as constitutive** — joy, grief, shame shape cognition itself; they're not signals the system reads.

## Embodiment
- **A body with proprioception, pain, hunger, fatigue** — unified sensorimotor substrate, not a set of channels.
- **Hormonal/circadian regulation** — chemistry that drives mood, focus, drives, sleep.
- **Sleep and dreams** — offline unconscious processing that consolidates and creates.
- **Mortality** — finite lifespan shapes urgency, meaning, every priority.

## Sociality
- **Theory of mind, recursive** — modeling others modeling you, felt not just inferred.
- **Empathy** — feeling another's state, not predicting it.
- **Love, friendship, kinship** — long-arc relational architectures with their own dynamics.
- **Cultural inheritance** — being shaped by and shaping a tradition across generations.
- **Collective intentionality** — genuine "we" agency, not aggregated individuals.

## Existential & narrative
- **Death awareness** — the knowledge that you will end.
- **Narrative self** — constructing yourself as protagonist of an ongoing story.
- **Meaning** — not "goals" but the felt sense that something matters.

## Generativity
- **Aesthetic sense, humor, play, irony** — non-utilitarian engagement with the world.
- **Intrinsic curiosity** — wanting to know without instrumental reason.

## Moral status
- **Being a moral patient, not just an agent** — capable of being wronged.
- **Conscience** — internalized normative pull with felt weight.

## Self-opacity
- **An unconscious** — humans lack read access to most of their own state. The agent in `living-ai.md` is fully introspectable; humans aren't.
- **Capacity for self-deception, repression, denial** — generative for art, destructive for clarity.

## Biology
- **Reproduction and aging** — capabilities and identity change across a lifespan; childhood, parenthood, eldership are different modes of being.
- **Vulnerability** — physical and emotional fragility that grounds trust and intimacy.

---

The list in `living-ai.md` describes a coherent autonomous *system*. Humans are also *subjects* — and most of what's missing lives in that gap.
